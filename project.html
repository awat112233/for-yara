About Us

Our story, mission and vision

Who We Are

Bright Technical and Vocational Institute (B.T.V.I) was founded in 2022 with a bold vision — to bridge the gap between education and industry by offering practical, career-focused technical training programs.

Located in the heart of Kurdistan, Bright TVI has grown into a leading vocational institution, recognized for academic excellence and commitment to student success.

Excellence Innovation Integrity Community
🎯

Our Mission

To provide high-quality technical and vocational education that equips students with the skills, knowledge, and character needed to thrive in the modern workforce.

🔭

Our Vision

To become the most respected technical institute in the region, known for producing graduates who lead industries and transform communities.

⭐

Our Values

We are committed to excellence, innovation, integrity, and creating an inclusive environment where every student can reach their full potential.

Admissions

Start your journey at Bright TVI

How to Apply

01

Choose Your Program

Browse our 6 departments and select the program that matches your passion and career goals.

02

Complete Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic information.

03

Submit Documents

Upload required documents including ID, previous certificates, and passport photo.

04

Entrance Assessment

Take the short entrance assessment to help us place you in the right program level.

05

Receive Decision

Get your admission decision within 5 business days via email and phone.

06

Enroll & Begin

Complete enrollment, pay tuition, and get ready for your first day at Bright TVI!

Requirements & Fees

📋 Requirements

  • Valid national ID or passport
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • 2 passport-size photos
  • Completed application form
  • Entrance assessment (online)

💰 Tuition & Fees

  • Registration Fee: 50,000 IQD
  • Semester Tuition: 350,000–500,000 IQD
  • Lab & Materials: 75,000 IQD/semester
  • Merit Scholarships Available
  • Installment Plans Available
